Grilled Asparagus Salad

Ingredients

  • Â¼ cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 12 fresh asparagus spears
  • 6 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon seasoned slivered almonds

Steps

  1. Preheat outdoor grill on low heat
  2. Combine oil and lemon juice in baking dish. Add asparagus and roll until coated.
  3. Combine spinach, Parmesan, and almonds in a bowl. Add asparagus with any oil-lemon juice mixture from the dish. Toss to blend and serve.

Simple healthy meal to make!

I love this recipe due to it being very healthy, and that it can be made in 15 mins. It is convenient and healthy cant go wrong with that.
